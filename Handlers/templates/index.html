What we're solving

Out at dinner with a bunch of friends and forgot to split the bill? Don't throw your credit card or cash in the middle of the table. Let us help you solve the hassle of dividing and paying for the bill.

How we're solving it

Take a picture of the receipt with your mobile device. All food items in the receipt are placed in a table where you can check off your friend's meal items. Provide your friend's phone number and we'll text them their portion of the bill along with a way to pay you.

Why we're solving it

You take care of the bill and also get reimbursed on the spot! Use the application and we can suggest restaurants and entrees to you based on your eating and spending habits, no matter where you are.
